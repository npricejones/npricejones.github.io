My goal is to leverage big data to provide new answers to old questions.

Big data has become ubiquitous in an age where collection points proliferate and storage is cheap. But even large datasets can have unexpected blindspots, and fully utilizing a dataset becomes ever more challenging as it grows. My astrophysics PhD has provided me with the ideal training to draw meaningful conclusions using population statistics, and I am excited to apply my training to fields outside of academia.
